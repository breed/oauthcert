How to get your public key

  1. Generate a WireGuard key pair using: wg genkey | tee privatekey | wg pubkey > publickey
  2. Copy the contents of the publickey file
  3. Paste it in the field above
  4. Keep your privatekey safe - you'll need to add it to your config
